Conclusion¶

There are still many problems not answer,such as Does the Pclass influence the survival? Does it revelant to the Fare ?
In the process of data investigation , as you diving in ,you 'll comefront many puzzles beyond your expectations.At first , we found the gender are different in survived and the dead . Considered the physical factor,then we study whether the age impact the survivals . according to the results , it seems doesn't much effect the survived. Given the missed values,we only can conclude that the age of survived male has a similar distribution with the deaded female,the survived female has a similar distributin with the deaded men. It also need to study whether they were might be a family ? It also need more reasearch. The Fare in survivals and victims were both in the majority, because there were more than 700 passengers have the fare under 100. Having high Fare passenger were rarely .Though browsing history ,I discovered the children and women were escaped firstly when the according happened.the age distribution doesn't has a positive distribution. During the Age study,the question about whether loss some survivors'age data still not clear.
